About Strango

Strango is a lightweight anonymous chat platform built for instant conversations without login requirements. The platform follows modern stranger-chat design principles focusing on privacy, minimalism, and global accessibility.

Mission

To create a secure environment for spontaneous conversations while respecting Indian digital laws and international online ethics frameworks.

Industry Standards

  • Minimal data architecture.
  • Real-time matching technology.
  • Mobile-friendly lightweight interface.

Compliance Awareness

Strango references Indian IT Act guidelines and United Nations digital responsibility principles to encourage safe communication culture.
