About Strango

Strango is a free anonymous chat platform focused on fast, private, and simple conversations. No login is required and chats are temporary.

Our Mission

We aim to provide a safe and lightweight stranger chat experience while respecting privacy and digital rights. Strango follows globally recognized principles such as the Universal Declaration of Human Rights (UDHR), especially freedom of expression balanced with user safety.

Legal Awareness

Strango operates as an intermediary platform under Indian law. Users must follow responsible online behaviour aligned with the Information Technology Act, 2000.
