Strango Privacy Policy

Strango is a lightweight anonymous chat platform designed to minimize data collection and protect user privacy. We aim to follow modern digital safety standards used across global communication platforms.

1. Data Collection

Strango does not require account registration or personal identity details. Chats are session-based and are not stored permanently on servers. Basic technical data such as connection status may be processed temporarily to maintain real-time matching.

2. Indian Legal Compliance

Strango aligns with general expectations under the Indian Information Technology Act 2000 and related intermediary guidelines by encouraging responsible user conduct and minimizing harmful content exposure.

3. International Privacy Principles

The platform follows widely accepted digital privacy principles inspired by United Nations recommendations on online safety, responsible communication, and respect for global digital rights.

4. User Responsibility

Users should avoid sharing sensitive personal information such as phone numbers, financial details, or identity documents during conversations.

5. Security Approach

Strango uses minimal data architecture and real-time communication methods to reduce long-term data exposure risks.

6. Contact

For privacy related questions, visit the Contact page from the main site footer.
