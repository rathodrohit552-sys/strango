Privacy Policy — Strango

Last Updated: 2026

Strango is an anonymous text chat platform designed to respect user privacy. We do not require login, personal identification, or account creation.

1. Information Collection

Strango does NOT collect personal identity data such as name, email, phone number, Aadhaar, or financial information. Temporary technical data like IP address may be processed for security, abuse prevention, and service stability.

2. Legal Compliance (India)

  • Information Technology Act, 2000
  • IT (Intermediary Guidelines and Digital Media Ethics Code) Rules, 2021
  • Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act)

3. International Privacy Principles

  • GDPR (European Union – General Data Protection Regulation)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • Global privacy-by-design principles

4. Cookies & Tracking

Strango uses minimal technical cookies strictly required for performance, security, and anonymous session handling.

5. Data Security

We apply encryption, secure socket communication, and monitoring to prevent misuse or unauthorized access.

6. User Rights

Users may stop using the service anytime. Since no accounts are created, no personal database profile exists.
