Strango – About, Privacy & Terms

About Strango

Strango is a lightweight anonymous stranger chat platform designed for fast, simple, and global conversations. The platform focuses on minimal design, real-time communication, and responsible digital interaction.

Our Mission

We aim to create a space where people can meet strangers, exchange ideas, and enjoy spontaneous conversations while maintaining respect and ethical online behavior.

Ethical Vision

  • Respectful communication
  • Safe anonymous interaction
  • Inclusive digital environment

Safety & Responsible Use

Strango encourages ethical digital behavior aligned with modern online standards.

  • Do not share personal or financial information.
  • Leave chats that feel uncomfortable.
  • Treat strangers with respect.
  • Anonymity should empower connection, not harm.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Strango is built to minimize personal data collection. We do not require real names, phone numbers, or identity verification for chat usage.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No mandatory personal accounts
  • No government ID or phone verification
  • No intentional sensitive tracking

Technical Data

Limited technical information such as temporary connection data or browser type may be processed to maintain stability, prevent misuse, and improve performance.

Cookies & Storage

Basic browser storage may be used to improve user experience and chat performance.

Indian Legal Awareness

Strango aims to follow responsible digital practices aligned with the Indian Information Technology Act, 2000 and general cyber safety expectations.

User Responsibility

Because chats are anonymous, users should avoid sharing personal identity or financial information. Strango cannot verify the identity of strangers.

Terms of Use

By using Strango, you agree to behave responsibly and follow ethical online communication standards.

Acceptable Use

  • No harassment, threats, or abusive behavior
  • No illegal content sharing
  • No scams or impersonation
  • No harmful activities

Indian Law Awareness

Users must comply with applicable Indian cyber laws, including principles of the Information Technology Act, 2000.

Anonymous Nature

Strango connects random users. We cannot control or verify the actions or behavior of strangers.

Limitation of Liability

Strango provides communication tools only and is not responsible for user-generated content.

Updates

These terms and policies may evolve to improve safety, performance, and compliance.
