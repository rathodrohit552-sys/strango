About Strango

Strango is a lightweight anonymous chat platform built for fast, private, and global conversations without login requirements.

Mission

To create a secure, privacy-focused environment where people can communicate freely while respecting digital laws.

Core Principles

  • Privacy First
  • No Personal Accounts
  • Global Accessibility
  • Fast Real-Time Matching

Compliance

Strango follows Indian IT laws and international privacy practices to maintain trust and safety.
