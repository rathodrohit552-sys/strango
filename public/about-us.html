About Strango

Strango is a modern anonymous chat platform focused on fast, lightweight, and privacy-first communication. The project explores minimal interface design used in contemporary stranger chat technologies.

Mission

Our mission is to create a simple environment where people can communicate freely without social pressure while maintaining respect for digital safety standards and international online conduct expectations.

Core Principles

  • Privacy-Focused Communication
  • No Mandatory Accounts
  • Global Accessibility
  • Real-Time Random Matching
  • Minimal Data Architecture

Industry Standards

Strango follows common UX practices seen in modern anonymous chat platforms, including lightweight performance, mobile responsiveness, and clear safety guidelines.

Compliance & Ethics

The platform encourages responsible usage aligned with Indian digital guidelines and international principles influenced by United Nations online safety recommendations.

Future Vision

Strango aims to evolve into a scalable global chat experience with strong focus on user safety, ethical communication, and transparent platform design.
