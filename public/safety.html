Safety Guidelines

Strango encourages safe and respectful anonymous communication. Our safety approach reflects Indian cyber safety recommendations and global online protection principles.

User Safety Tips

  • Do not share personal identity, phone number, or address
  • Disconnect immediately if you feel uncomfortable
  • Report harmful behaviour through contact channels

Legal Awareness

Users should understand that Indian cyber laws prohibit harassment, threats, or illegal content distribution online.

Global Digital Rights

Strango supports United Nations principles promoting safe digital environments, responsible online behaviour, and protection from abuse.
