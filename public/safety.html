Safety Guidelines

Strango encourages responsible communication following international online safety standards and Indian cyber safety advisories.

Indian Cyber Safety Practices

Users should follow advisories issued under India’s cybercrime prevention framework. Avoid sharing OTPs, passwords, or financial information.

UN Online Safety Principles

We promote respectful digital interaction consistent with UN recommendations for safe online communities and protection from abuse.

Tips for Safe Chatting

  • Disconnect if uncomfortable
  • Never reveal personal identity
  • Report suspicious behaviour to authorities
