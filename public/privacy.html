Privacy Policy

Strango is designed to provide anonymous communication while respecting user privacy. We follow principles aligned with the Indian Information Technology Act, 2000 and global privacy expectations.

Data Collection

Strango does not require account registration. We do not store personal identity information. Messages are session-based and not permanently saved.

Indian Law Compliance

Our platform operates in alignment with India’s Information Technology Act, 2000 and applicable intermediary guidelines. Users are responsible for lawful behaviour while using the service.

International Standards

We follow general privacy principles consistent with UN human rights frameworks such as Article 12 of the Universal Declaration of Human Rights regarding privacy protection.

User Responsibility

Users should never share personal details such as phone numbers, addresses, or financial data while chatting.
